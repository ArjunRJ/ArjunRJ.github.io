About Me

I am an experienced Android developer and a longtime GNU/Linux geek interested in the newest technologies and trends. I have obtained a bachelor's degree from the Shoolini University of Technology in Computer science and engineering. During my studies, I have been acquiring work experience in various fields and lately I have been concentrating on software development on the Android platform and some .NET , angular, Ionic, javascript , NodeJs . I have in total over three years of active Android development experience. I have worked on numerous projects used by hundreds of thousands of people. TheTeacherapp, Uhabit, Firki are some of them. I am eager to constantly learn new things. At the moment, my focus is on the new Java 8 and 9 language features, RxJava and RxAndroid, Kotlin, architectural patterns on Android (MVVM, MVP, etc.) and of course I am always up for trying out the newest Android libraries.
